Ham Sandwich

We will now show you how to construct the most amazing ham and cheese you've ever had. The ham and cheese was popularized in 1894 when they were the only food being sold in New York baseball parks. The origins of the sandwich are believed to have come from an 18th century Irish immigrant.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish mustard
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onions
  • 1 teaspoon poppy seeds
  • 1 teaspoon dill seed
  • 8 slices swiss cheese
  • 8 slices cooked ham
  • 8 hamburger buns

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 250 degrees F
  2. Combine butter,mustard,onions,poppy seeds and dill seed into a bowl. Spread the insides of each bun with this mixture. Place a slice of cheese and ham inside each bun
  3. Wrap the buns in foil and place in a preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes until the cheese has melted

Grilled Cheese

Wowee we're gonna show you how to make the most amazing grilled cheese you've ever had. Humans have been combining bread and cheese together since the dawn of time. There's evidence of the ancient romans putting together bread with melted cheese. However the modern american grilled cheese was only created pretty recenttly in 1916 when James Kraft invented a way to process cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese
  • 3 tablespoons butter

Directions

  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat
  2. Butter one slice of bread then place the bread butter down on the skillet and add a slice of cheese.
  3. Butterthe second slice slice of bread and place it in the skillet. Grill until lightly brown and cheese is melted

Sloppy Joe

It'll always be a great time when you whip out these sandwiches at a party. In the early and mid 20th century there were plenty of variations of the "sloppy joe" such as the chopped meat sandwich, spanish hamburgers, hamburg a la creole, and minced beef spanish style. It is theorized that these sandwiches sold in Iowa in the 1930s were popularized by a cook named Joe.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds ground beef
  • 1 cup chopped yellow onion
  • 1 cup finely chopped celery
  • 1 bottle tomato based chili sauce
  • Buns

Directions

  1. Crumble the ground beef into a pot over medium high heat and cook until it is evenly browned
  2. Drain out the grease and add the onion and celery and cook until the onions are tender
  3. Reduce the heat to medium and then pour in the chili sauce. Simmer until it is thick and serve on the buns
